Discover Taniti Island

Welcome to Taniti, located in the Pacific Ocean. With a history dating back to the early 1800s, Taniti has been a popular destination for tourists seeking white sand beaches, turquoise waters, and a relaxing beache time. Visitors can explore historical landmarks and hike beautiftl trails while also enjoying modern amenities like luxury resorts, shopping centers, and golf courses. The island also boasts lush tropical forests, crystal-clear lagoons, and a diverse array of marine life, making it a top destination for snorkeling, diving, and other water sports. Come discover Paradise Island and experience the beauty, culture, and relaxation that it has to offer.

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific. While the island has an area of less than 500 square miles, the terrain is varied and includes both sandy and rocky beaches, a small but safe harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano. Taniti has an indigenous population of about 20,000. Until a recent increase in tourism, most the Tanitian economy was dominated by fishing or agriculture.
