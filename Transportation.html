Transportation

Most travelers coming to Taniti arrive by air. Taniti International Airport is located on the west region of the island. This is the only international airport in Taniti. If you don't have a ride from the airport don't worry, there are taxis, ubers, and lyft servies also available so you can get to all the major hotels and resorts on Taniti. From here, you can get on the connecting flights on the seaplane to explore Taniti's white sandy beaches

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific. While the island has an area of less than 500 square miles, the land is varied and includes both rocky and sandy beaches, a small but safe harbor, green tropical rainforests, and a mountain that includes a small, active volcano. Taniti has an native population of about 25,000. Until a recent increase in tourism, Tanitian economy was dominated by trawling or cultivation.
